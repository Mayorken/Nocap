**Description**

Social accountability challenges where friends lock a refundable commitment, show proof, and settle transparently on Monad.

**Problem**

Goals announced in my group chats usually disappear after a few days. My friends and I say we will study, build, create content, work out, or save consistently, but the promise has no meaningful accountability and everyone quietly stops checking in.

**Solution**

NoCap turns a short group goal into an on-chain pact. Every participant locks the same small commitment, submits proof that they completed the challenge, and receives a payout after the host verifies the results. Successful participants recover their commitment and share forfeited commitments. The rules and settlement are visible on Monad rather than controlled by a private group admin.

## Three-minute demo script

### 0:00–0:20 — Personal problem

“My friends and I announce goals in group chats all the time. We say we’ll study, work out, ship content, or build every day. A few days later, everybody quietly stops. NoCap gives those promises real accountability.”

### 0:20–0:40 — Product

“A NoCap challenge is a short on-chain pact. Everyone locks the same refundable commitment. Show proof and finish the pact to recover your commitment and share what was forfeited.”

### 0:40–1:15 — Create

1. Connect the host wallet.
2. Create **Ship Something Today**.
3. Set days to `0` for the three-minute demo duration.
4. Set the commitment to a very small testnet amount.
5. Confirm the real Monad transaction.

### 1:15–1:45 — Join and prove

1. Switch to the second wallet.
2. Open the live challenge and join it.
3. Select a proof screenshot.
4. Submit the proof transaction.

### 1:45–2:20 — Verify and settle

1. Switch back to the host wallet.
2. Approve the participant’s proof.
3. After the three-minute deadline, settle the pact.
4. Switch to the successful participant and claim the payout.

### 2:20–2:50 — Trust

Show the deployed contract and transactions in the Monad explorer. Explain that NoCap does not custody the commitments and that the current MVP uses host review, with squad quorum planned next.

### 2:50–3:00 — Close

“NoCap turns ‘I’ll start tomorrow’ into a pact your squad can verify. Lock in. Show proof. Stay consistent.”
